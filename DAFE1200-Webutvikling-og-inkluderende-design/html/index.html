Dyslexia? Reading made easy

When text is created without the intent of dyslexics reading it, there becomes a problem. Thats why we created Alexia, the application that helps you with your dyslexia.

When life gives you dyslexia, Alexia is here to save the day

With just one click you can turn unclear texts to readable ones

Problem

Learning about the importance of accessibility in our lectures, made us think about what other people might experience.
We started thinking alot about the reading disability called dyslexia. This condition makes it harder for dyslexics to read. It could vary between blurriness, recognition of letters and numbers, difficulty of pronouncing words and a lot more.

Solution

Our Solution is to make an app that can scan or take pictures of signs, texts, menus or anything that has text on it. It's as easy as opening the app and hovering your phone over the text you want to read. If you take a picture of the text, it transfers it to a textbox, rather than the original background of the text. Here you'll also have the choice to change the font.

Learn More

Our idea

The app Alexia can help dyslexic people to better read and understand texts they come across in their daily lives. This doesnt 'heal' dyslexia, but we hope it can make dyslexics lives better
